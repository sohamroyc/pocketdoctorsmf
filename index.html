First Aid Guide

Essential emergency response procedures for common injuries

🔥

Burns

Cool with running water for 10-20 minutes

⚠️ Don't: Use ice, butter, or break blisters

💧

Bleeding

Apply direct pressure with clean cloth

⚠️ Don't: Remove embedded objects

💚

CPR

30 compressions, 2 breaths

⚠️ Remember: Call emergency services first

🤢

Choking

5 back blows, 5 abdominal thrusts

⚠️ For infants: Use back blows and chest thrusts

🦴

Fractures

Immobilize the injured area

⚠️ Don't: Try to straighten the bone

🌡️

Heat Stroke

Move to cool area, remove clothing

⚠️ Emergency: Call 108 immediately
